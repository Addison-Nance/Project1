What are the Symptoms of COVID-19?

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), the most common symptoms of COVID-19 are:

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry cough

These symptoms are usually mild and occur gradually while some people exhibit no sympoms at all. Some cases have shown that aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat, and diarrhea can also be symptoms.

Testing Resources

Have symptoms of COVID-19? Been in contact with someone who has the Coronavirus? Visit the Center for Disease control's website for testing resources.
